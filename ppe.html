Quality personal protective equipment for every workplace — keeping your team safe and compliant.

Workplace Safety Starts with the Right PPE

Personal Protective Equipment is not optional — it's a legal and ethical obligation. Whether you're running a construction site, a factory floor, a laboratory, or a healthcare facility, protecting your team with the right gear is non-negotiable.

At RDS, we supply a comprehensive range of PPE and protective clothing to businesses across South Africa. From basic hard hats to full-body protective suits, we ensure your workforce stays safe, compliant, and productive.

Head & Face Protection

Hard hats, safety helmets, face shields, and eye protection designed to protect workers from impact, flying debris, and chemical splashes.

  • Construction Hard Hats (SANS certified)
  • Full-Face Shields
  • Safety Goggles & Glasses
  • Welding Helmets & Masks
  • Bump Caps for Light Industry
  • Anti-Dust Face Masks (FFP1 / FFP2 / N95)

High-Visibility Clothing

Reflective vests, jackets, and bibs that ensure workers are seen in low-light and high-traffic environments.

  • Hi-Vis Reflective Vests (Class 2 & 3)
  • Hi-Vis Jackets & Hoodies
  • Hi-Vis Trousers & Overalls
  • Reflective Strips & Accessories
  • Hi-Vis Rain Gear
  • Branded & Custom Hi-Vis Options

Hand & Arm Protection

  • Latex & Nitrile Disposable Gloves

  • Heat & Welding Gloves
  • Anti-Vibration Gloves
  • General-Purpose Work Gloves

Body & Foot Protection

Overalls, coveralls, safety boots, and gumboots engineered for protection in industrial, construction, and chemical environments.

  • Cotton & Poly-Cotton Overalls

Hearing Protection

Earplugs and earmuffs for environments where noise levels exceed safe working limits, protecting long-term hearing health.

  • Foam Disposable Earplugs
  • Reusable Earplugs with Cord

Respiratory Protection

  • Respirator Cartridges (Gas / Vapour / Combination)

  • Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR)
