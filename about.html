Who We Are

Rialivhuwa Diversified Solutions (RDS) is a proudly South African supplier dedicated to equipping businesses with everything they need to operate safely, professionally, and efficiently.

We specialise in four core areas: Protective Clothing (PPE), Cleaning Chemicals & Machines, Branding, and Stationery & Office Supplies. From a single-person home office to a large enterprise, we tailor our offering to suit your needs and budget.

Based in South Africa, we serve clients across all industries — including education, healthcare, construction, retail, and corporate sectors — delivering quality products at competitive prices, with service that goes the extra mile.
